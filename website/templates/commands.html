Commands

Everything you can do with HockeyBot

/help <menu>

Display the help menu for a specific category.

/info

View info about the bot — version, stats, links.

/avatar

Get a user's avatar (or the bot's own avatar).

/suggest <suggestion>

Submit a feature request or improvement idea.

/player <name>

Fetch stats and info for any active NHL player.

/team <abbreviation>

Get full team info — record, division, roster links.

/teams

List all 32 NHL teams with abbreviations.

/standings

Current Eastern & Western Conference standings.

/schedule <abbreviation>

Upcoming schedule for any NHL team.

/game <abbreviation>

Live/upcoming game info for a team.

/today

All games happening today.

/tomorrow

All games happening tomorrow.

/yesterday

Results from yesterday's games.

/set-schedule-channel <channel>

Auto-post daily game schedules to a channel. Admin

/remove-schedule-channel

Disable the daily schedule auto-post. Admin

/guess-the-player

Guess a random NHL player from clues.

/trivia

Answer an NHL trivia question to earn points.

/leaderboard <trivia|gtp|fantasy>

View top players for trivia, guess-the-player, or fantasy.

/mypoints <trivia|gtp|fantasy>

Check your own point totals.

/suggest-trivia <question> <answer>

Submit a trivia question for the bot's question bank.

/my-roster

View your active teams, bench, total points, and swap count.

/swap-teams

Use one of your 10 seasonal swaps to change a team.

/ace-team

Pick your weekly x3 points multiplier team.

/leaderboard fantasy

See the full Fantasy League leaderboard.

/mypoints fantasy

Check your current Fantasy League point total.

/warn <member> <reason>

Warn a member and log the action. Mod

/punishments <member>

View a member's punishment history (90-day limit on Free tier). Mod

/add-note <member> <note>

Add a private staff note to a user's record. Mod

/export-logs

Download your server's full moderation history. 💎 Referee Only
